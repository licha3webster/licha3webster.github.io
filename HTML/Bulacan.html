    Festivals of Bulacan

    Bulacan's festivals are vibrant celebrations rooted in faith, culture, and revolutionary pride. The Obando Fertility Rites draw thousands of devotees in a centuries-old dancing procession dedicated to Saints Clare, Paschal Baylon, and the Virgin of Salambao. The Bocaue River Festival features a dramatic fluvial parade honoring the Holy Cross. The Singkaban Festival in Malolos showcases bamboo craft artistry and cultural performances celebrating Bulacan's heritage. These festivals reflect the province's deep spirituality, artistic soul, and community spirit.
